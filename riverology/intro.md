# Riverology

Real‑time river and stream toolkit for open worlds. Build natural rivers with spline‑based flow, performant materials, and buoyancy interaction.

## What you get

- **Flow‑based physics** for convincing river behavior.
- **Optimized splines** to author complex courses and tributaries.
- **Buoyancy volumes** for boats, props, and gameplay.
- Designed to coexist with Oceanology for cohesive oceans‑to‑rivers workflows.

## Who is it for

Teams that need controllable rivers for open worlds, simulation, or gameplay.
