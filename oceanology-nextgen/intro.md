# Oceanology NextGen

![oceanology-nextgen](intro/NextGenIntro_01.jpg)

Oceanology NextGen is a professional water solution for Unreal Engine. It focuses on predictable performance and clear controls so teams ship reliable results without surprises.

## Who it’s for
Studios building open‑worlds, cinematics, or multiplayer projects that demand scale and realism.

## Highlights
- Hybrid spectral waves (Gerstner + FFT) with math‑based coastal breakers.
- C++ quadtree tessellation, World Partition, and network replication.
- Multiple water bodies including optimized lake support.
- Flow‑based foam, crest splashes, underwater volumetrics, and god rays.
- RVT landscape integration and Niagara‑driven displacement FX.
- Preset Data Assets and flexible appearance from photoreal to stylized.
- Engineered for high‑end GPUs; built on a mature, production‑proven C++ core.

## Requirements
- Unreal Engine 5.6 or newer.
- Windows with DirectX 12 and Shader Model 6 enabled.
- High‑end GPU recommended.
