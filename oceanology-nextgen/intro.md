# Oceanology NextGen

Ocean rendering and underwater toolkit for Unreal Engine **5.5+**. Built for real‑time worlds and production teams that need predictable setup, fast iteration, and clean integration.

## What you get

- Physically inspired ocean surface with **quadtree tessellation** and infinite extent.
- **Buoyancy** and flow interactions for actors and boats.
- **Underwater** effects, **caustics**, volumetric fog, shoreline wetness, and crest splash.
- Niagara integration and sample content to get started fast.
- Compatible with Water Bodies and world partition workflows.

> See the feature list for reference: Audio, Beaufort Scale, Buoyancy, Caustics, Flow Physics, Height Data, Infinite Ocean, Niagara, Oceanology Manager, Quadtree Tessellation, Shore Waves, Shoreline Wetness, State Transition, Swimming, Underwater, Volumetric Fog, Water Bodies, Water Mask, Water Volume, Wave Crest Splash.

## Who is it for

Teams building games, simulations, and cinematics that need controllable oceans, underwater scenes, and performant large worlds.
