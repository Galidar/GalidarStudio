Welcome to the official Galidar Studio roadmap! This is where we share upcoming features, development updates, and release notes for our Unreal Engine water simulation plugins.

<!-- truncate -->

## What We Build

Galidar Studio specializes in professional water systems for Unreal Engine 5:

- **Oceanology Pro** (NextGen) — The complete water package: FFT + Spectral Gerstner + Gerstner waves, rivers, lakes, and breaking waves for both games and cinematics
- **Oceanology Lite** (Legacy) — Performance-optimized ocean and lakes for mid-range hardware and wide compatibility
- **Riverology** — Spline-based rivers with realistic flow physics and exclusive river features

:::note Product Naming
Our products are being renamed with the **Oceanology Pro 2.0** release (Q3 2026): Legacy → **Lite**, NextGen → **Pro**. No re-purchase needed for existing customers.
[Read the announcement →](/roadmap/product-rebranding-announcement)
:::
